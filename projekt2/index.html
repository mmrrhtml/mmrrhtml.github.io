Elephant

This project was a part of the EDITORIAL GRAPHIC workshops in School of Form. The main task was to design four covers of magazines. We were able to choose from a few titles. I chose Elephant magazine. It's about ceramics. Apart form design layout, we also had to design logo. My logo combines both the figure of an elephant and typography. My covers reference to water patterns.
